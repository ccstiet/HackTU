ABOUT

TIET

The Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology (TIET) is one of India's oldest and finest educational institutions, which provides a steady source of highly skilled talent to the nation as well as overseas. Founded in 1956, Thapar Institute of Engineering & Technology, located in Patiala, has been a pioneer in engineering education, research and innovation. Our community involves ingenious minds solvers who are eager to make the world a better place to live in with their innovative techniques and discoveries.

CCS

The Creative Computing Society is one of the oldest and most prestigious societies of TIET. It consists of budding tech geeks who are constantly working towards building their skill set and creating and promoting a healthy technical environment at TIET. With the knowledge and experience of 15 years, the society aims to create and promote a healthy technical environment at TIET.

FAQS

These are some few common questions. If you have any other questions, please get in touch.

  • General Questions
When?

HackTU will be a 24 hours hackathon which will take place in the month of November. Stay tuned for exact dates.

Where?

HackTu will be held at Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology, Patiala

Who should attend?

This is a student hackathon and only students are allowed to participate.

Is wifi available?

Yes, constant internet connection will be provided.

Can I register individually?

No, Team is mandatory.

How big can my team be?

Team must consist of 4 team members max and 2 min

Will there be food arrangement?

Yes. Absolutely. You will be provided with proper meals and refreshments. Thanks to our sponsors.

What are the criteria of short listing the teams?

The registered participants will be shortlisted on the basis of their GitHub and LinkedIn profiles.

What do I bring with me to the event?

You are required to bring your school/college ID, laptops, phone, chargers and extension board.

Is there reimbursement for travel?

No, we do not offer any reimbursement for travel expenses.
